The purpose of this dashboard is to visualize the number and severity of vehicle collisions in Seattle, and to explore how they are distributed across the city. The map shows all collisions from the year 2025, with different colors representing severity levels, and sizes representing the number of people involved. The chart breaks down the severity distribution for the currently visible collisions on the map.
